How I work

  • Diagnosis before anything else

    Before opening Figma or writing a line of code, I dig into the business, the market and what is holding it back. Most digital problems have strategic roots, not aesthetic ones.

  • Design and development in one workflow

    There is no handoff. Design and code evolve together, which cuts rework and shortens the timeline.

  • Delivery with documentation

    Every project ships with a design system, a usage guide and a structured handoff. You never depend on the studio for small changes.
