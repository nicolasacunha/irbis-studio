What a SaaS needs first

A SaaS needs a landing page that converts before anything else. Most teams launch the product without fixing their external messaging, and lose trials to confusion, not to missing features.

The landing page is the front door. It has to answer in 5 seconds what the product does, who it's for, and what happens after the trial. No ambiguity, no feature dump.
