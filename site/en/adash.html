ADASH

Year 2026
Service Product Design + Dev + AI
Stack React · TypeScript · Vite
Deliverable Custom SaaS dashboard

The client ran their entire marketing and sales operation across four tools that never talked to each other. Meta Ads in one place, CRM in another, content in Google Drive, reports in Excel. Buying decisions depended on data that always arrived late. The problem was never a lack of information. It was the lack of one unified view.

app.adash.com.br
The Scenario

Four
tools.
Zero
visibility.

The team burned hours every week just consolidating information across platforms. Reports arrived late, decisions ran on stale data. We could have wired up a few APIs and shipped a generic panel. That was the fast path. Instead, we mapped how the operation actually worked: how the team thought, the order they made decisions in, the terms they used day to day. ADASH was built around their logic, not the logic of the tools they already had. That difference separates a dashboard people use from one they call the wrong tool a month later.
