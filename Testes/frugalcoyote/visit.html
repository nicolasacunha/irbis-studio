Donations

Your gently used things fund the whole operation.

The shop is stocked entirely by donations from Borrego Springs and its visitors. If it's clean, working, and ready for a second life, the Coyote wants it.

What sells well here

  • Men's & women's clothing, shoes, and bags
  • Jewelry — costume to collectible
  • Kitchenware and small housewares
  • Books, records, and DVDs
  • Hand tools and yarn
  • Furniture, lamps, paintings, and art

How to donate

  • Bring items during shop hours, Tue–Sat 8am–4pm
  • Call ahead for furniture or a full carload: (760) 767-4608
  • Items should be clean and in working order
  • Ask for your tax-deductible receipt at drop-off

Frugal Coyote is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit — your donation of gently used goods comes with a tax-deductible receipt, gratefully written on the spot.

Volunteering

The shop runs on neighbors.

Sorting donations, styling the racks, working the register, chatting with visitors from all over — volunteers keep the Coyote howling. If you've got a few hours a week and a soft spot for a good find, come meet the crew.

Want in?

Stop by the shop during open hours and say you'd like to help, or call and ask about volunteering.
