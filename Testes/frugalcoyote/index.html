Where the money goes

Every purchase funds Borrego.

Frugal Coyote is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit run under the Borrego Valley Endowment Fund. The proceeds from your five-dollar find go to work a few blocks away.

1. A neighbor donates

Someone in Borrego drops off a box of things ready for a second life.

2. You score a find

That jacket, lamp, or stack of records rings up at the register.

3. Borrego feels it

Proceeds flow through the endowment fund into programs for kids, seniors, and neighbors in need.

Proceeds support

  • Borrego Springs Youth & Seniors Center
  • Children's Center Programs
  • Borrego Springs Community Resource Center
  • + other local causes

The Frugal Coyote and its charitable giving are managed by the Borrego Valley Endowment Fund.
