The designer

Trained at the cutting bench

Brooklyn→ Industrial engineering→ R. Gems apprenticeship→ Diamond District

Alison Lin learned lapidary at R. Gems, one of the world's leading gem companies in the Diamond District — working with some of the finest stones in the industry before designing a single ring.

Every collection starts with her childhood memories, blending that nostalgia with modern elegance. Each piece is hand-crafted locally in New York City.
