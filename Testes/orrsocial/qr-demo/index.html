Under the hood

  • Instant: one file, zero external requests — no fonts, no scripts, no images to wait for.
  • One job: a single CTA. No menu, no detours between the scan and the offer.
  • Trackable: every printed QR carries its own tags — ?utm_source=qr&utm_medium=print&utm_campaign=… — so each table tent, sticker or insert reports scans separately in analytics.
